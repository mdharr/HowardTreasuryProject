Welcome, traveler, to The Robert E. Howard Treasury, a digital haven that echoes with the reverberations of Howard's mighty pen. Here, in this sanctuary of literary wonders, we have gathered the treasures of the master himself – tales of adventure, fantasy, and unbridled imagination.

In the ageless volumes of Del Rey books, Howard's legacy endures. With every word he penned, he breathed life into bold heroes and dark villains, conjured untamed landscapes, and wove intricate plots that continue to captivate minds across generations. He was a bard, a visionary, and a trailblazer of speculative fiction.

As you explore the depths of this treasury, you shall find excerpts from the entire compendium of Howard's works, a testament to his versatility and mastery. Each passage, a gateway to adventure; every stanza, a portal to the fantastic. Perchance, a whispered verse or a thunderous clash of steel shall ignite your fascination.

Feel the primal pulse of Conan the Cimmerian, the indomitable spirit of Solomon Kane, and the chilling embrace of Bran Mak Morn. Howard's characters are not mere ink and paper; they are eternal companions on the literary journey.

But the treasures of this realm extend beyond mere words. Forge your own path by registering an account, and become a curator of your own collections, echoing Howard's enduring spirit. Create and manage, share and cherish – in this digital age, the legacy of the master lives on, enshrined in your hands.

Robert E. Howard's influence reverberates in the souls of authors, artists, and dreamers worldwide. His impact is etched in the annals of fantasy literature, an enduring flame that guides the path of those who dare to dream. Through his words, worlds have been born, and echoes of his tales continue to shape our imagination.
